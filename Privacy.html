Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 21, 2026

Overview

This Custom GPT helps create video scripts and generate videos using the HeyGen API. When a user asks the GPT to create a video, the text needed to create that video may be sent to HeyGen.

Information Sent to HeyGen

The GPT may send video prompts, scripts, production instructions, style preferences, and related video-generation details to HeyGen. This may include any information the user chooses to include in the video request.

Information We Do Not Intentionally Collect

We do not intentionally collect payment information, government identification numbers, health records, passwords, or other sensitive personal information through this GPT. Users should avoid including sensitive personal information in video prompts.

How Information Is Used

Information is used only to create, manage, and retrieve HeyGen video outputs requested by the user.

Third-Party Services

Video generation is provided by HeyGen. Information sent to HeyGen is subject to HeyGen’s own terms and privacy practices.

Data Retention

This GPT does not operate a separate database or intentionally store user-submitted prompts outside the ChatGPT conversation and the HeyGen services used to generate videos.

User Responsibility

Users are responsible for ensuring they have the right to submit any scripts, images, names, likenesses, trademarks, or other content used in video-generation requests.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact the operator of this GPT.
